Acme Corp Employee HR Policy

Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave, 6 days of casual leave, and 12 days of sick leave per year. Sick leave beyond 2 consecutive days requires a medical certificate. Maternity leave is 26 weeks. Paternity leave is 5 days. All leaves must be applied at least 3 days in advance via the HR portal.

Work From Home

Work from home is allowed up to 2 days per week with manager approval. Full-time remote work is not permitted without department head and HR approval. Employees must be available on communication channels from 10 AM to 5 PM IST on WFH days.

Reimbursements

Local travel reimbursement is capped at INR 2,000 per day. Outstation hotel stays are reimbursed at up to INR 10,000 per day. Meals during official trips are reimbursed up to INR 800 per day. All reimbursements must be claimed within 30 days with receipts.

Payroll

Salaries are paid on the last working day of every month. Salary slips are available on the self-service portal from the 1st of the following month. Discrepancies must be reported within 7 days of credit.

IT and Laptop Support

New employees receive a laptop within 3 working days. For laptop issues, raise a ticket on the IT helpdesk portal or email it-support@acmecorp.com. Critical replacements are provided within 2 working days.

Office Hours

Standard office hours are 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM IST, Monday to Friday. Core hours are 10 AM to 5 PM.
